Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Agile Poker respects your privacy. This application is designed to operate without collecting or storing personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No cookies
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No IP address logging
  • No session recording

Temporary Data

Room data (player names, votes, story titles) exists only in memory for the duration of a session and is discarded when the session ends or the server restarts.

Third-Party Services

Agile Poker does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking platforms.

Security

All communication is encrypted using HTTPS and secure WebSockets (WSS).

Contact

For privacy-related concerns, please contact the site administrator.
